Artist Portfolio Site

Vincent van Gogh


This is a passion project meant to act as a sample of my work. It is a homepage demo of an artist portfolio website for the late Vincent van Gogh. Because of the copyright status of his work, I was able to have fun creating in lieu of legal agreements.


Objectives:

  • Implement a fully-responsive and creative home for his work
  • Provide a simple UI that promotes purchasing and user retention
  • Keep users engaged with unique displays and interactivity
  • Balance a simple UI with unique, eye-catching interactivity


view the live site github repository

Ideation &
Prototyping

Since this is a new site, I did not have a website to improve upon. I first thought of what the client would need the website to contain, the most important being: a collection of his work, ecommerce capabilities for selling prints, and information about gallery shows. My goals with the UI were to make something eyecatching and creative while also keeping the user in mind and creating an ease of usability.

Style

I based the style of the website off of the nature of van Gogh's work. I pulled colors and motion from his paintings to incorporate into the typography and components of the site. I focused particularly on Wheat Field With Cypresses because I believed this painting to be representative of his work in terms of color and brush stroke. I also picked a background color representative on archival canvas.
